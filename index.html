Our Story

Founders Philosophy

Founded by Mr. R. Balasubramanian in 1988, Apex Precision started as a small CNC workshop with a vision to put Coimbatore on the global manufacturing map. Today, we stand as a beacon of quality and precision in the industrial sector.

Our philosophy is simple: **Zero Defect, Global Delivery**. We invest heavily in R&D and human capital to ensure every component leaving our facility meets the stringent requirements of international standards.

"Quality is not an act, it is a habit. At Apex, we breathe precision in every micron."
